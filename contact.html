Frequently Asked Questions

How can I participate in RISE UP programs?

You can participate by registering for our programs through the Programs page, volunteering your time and skills, or partnering with us as an organization. Visit our Get Involved page for more details.

Are the programs free for students?

Yes, most of our programs are free for students. We believe in equal access to education and opportunities. Some specialized programs may have minimal fees to cover materials, but scholarships are always available.

How can my organization partner with RISE UP?

We welcome partnerships with educational institutions, corporations, and NGOs. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@theriseup.org or fill out the partnership form on our Get Involved page.

What types of volunteer opportunities are available?

We offer various volunteer roles including mentoring, workshop facilitation, content creation, event organization, and administrative support. You can choose roles that match your skills and availability.

How can I support RISE UP financially?

You can support us through one-time or recurring donations. Your contributions help provide scholarships, program materials, and resources for students in need. Visit our Get Involved page for donation options.
