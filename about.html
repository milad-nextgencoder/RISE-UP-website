About RISE UP

Building a future where every student has equal opportunities to succeed and lead

Our Mission

To empower students through quality education, reduce inequalities, and build a culture of peace and justice among youth worldwide.

Our Vision

A world where every young person has access to quality education and opportunities to become leaders and change-makers in their communities.

Our Values

Resilience, Inspiration, Solidarity, and Empowerment - the core principles that guide everything we do.

Who We Are

RISE UP is an international educational and social organization strategically aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals 4 (Quality Education), 10 (Reduced Inequalities), and 16 (Peace, Justice, and Strong Institutions).

Founded on the principles of educational equity and social justice, we work tirelessly to create equal learning opportunities, reduce educational disparities, and promote a culture of peace and justice among youth globally.

Our Approach

Through comprehensive training programs, innovative social projects, and strategic partnerships with Albukhary International University and other local and global institutions, we empower students to build sustainable pathways for personal growth, leadership development, and positive community impact.
